# VPN (Virtual Private Network)

## Objective

Understand the purpose of VPNs and their use in secure remote access.

---

## What is a VPN?

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) creates an encrypted connection between your device and a remote network.

---

## Benefits

- Encrypts Internet traffic
- Protects privacy
- Enables secure remote work
- Access internal company resources

---

## Common Uses

- Remote employees
- Secure public Wi-Fi connections
- Access company systems

---

## Common VPN Protocols

- OpenVPN
- WireGuard
- IKEv2
- L2TP/IPsec

---

- VPN
