# No Sound Troubleshooting

## Problem

No audio output from speakers or headphones.

---

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Check volume level.
2. Verify output device.
3. Restart Windows Audio Service.
4. Reinstall audio driver.
5. Test another headset.

---

## Tools Used

- Device Manager
- Services
- Sound Settings

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Driver Troubleshooting
